IT Support & Help Desk

CompTIA A+ is the starting point for an IT career. Covering hardware, software, networking, troubleshooting, and security, A+ validates the skills employers need most. CompTIA Network+
Networking

CompTIA Network+ is the premier certification for validating essential networking knowledge and skills. It covers network connectivity, documentation, service configuration, data centres, cloud, virtual networking, monitoring, troubleshooting, and security hardening — and is the only industry certification covering both wired and wireless networks. It prepares professionals for roles in technical support, network operations, and system administration.

CompTIA Security+
Cybersecurity

CompTIA Security+ is the most trusted entry-level cybersecurity certification, establishing the essential skills required for core security functions. It validates practical, hands-on abilities in securing networks, applications, and devices while ensuring data integrity, confidentiality, and availability. Globally recognised and vendor-neutral, it is the first security certification IT professionals should earn and serves as a springboard to intermediate-level cybersecurity roles.
